Effective Date: May 15, 2026

ArtermisX ("we", "us", or "our") provides QR Scanner New (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

QR Scanner New is designed to work primarily on your device.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not intentionally collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or other personal information directly from you.

However, the App may process the following information locally on your device:

- QR code and barcode scan results
- QR codes you create
- QR Wallet items saved by you
- Scan and creation history
- Text, website URLs, email data, contact data, Wi-Fi data, event data, or other information you enter into the App

This information is used to provide the App’s features and is stored locally on your device unless a specific feature requires sharing through your device’s standard share options.

## 2. Camera Permission

The App requests camera access so you can scan QR codes and barcodes.

Camera data is used for scanning only. We do not use the camera to identify you, and we do not intentionally upload camera images or video to our servers.

You can disable camera permission at any time in your device settings, but scanning features may not work without it.

## 3. QR Wallet and History

The App may store QR Wallet items and scan or creation history on your device.

This data helps you review previous scans, manage saved QR codes, rename QR Wallet items, and access QR content later.

If you uninstall the App or clear the App’s data, locally stored QR Wallet and history data may be deleted.
